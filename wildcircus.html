Presentation of Wild Circus International

Come discover one of the famous circus show in the world. Enter in the magical world of the Wild Circus. This show will take you to the border of reality with outstanding performance, fairy decoration straight out of your imagination and amazing music.

This show will transport you beyond your dreams and your spirit. Artists will make you live an extraordinary moment through their performances.

You can discover three performances of the show below.

Discover the ZATREPE troop

During this show, let yourself carried by this fabulous show filled with lightness and agility

The ZATREPE troop offers you a fabulous performance during wich you will discover incredible acrobatics.

And CELESTE our elephant

Celeste will show you all her agility, strength and softness. You can discover her walking on a ball but also raised on her front legs.

You will appreciate all the finesse and complicity between CELESTE and IELENA her trainer.

Come and laugh with FRAPPADINGO

FRAPPADINGO will bring back the good mood and the smile to the whole family with their crazy performances.

Come and discover their universe filled with simplicty
